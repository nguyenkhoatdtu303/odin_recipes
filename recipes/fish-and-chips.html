Fish And Chips

Description

This is a recipe to cook a tradional British dish "fish and chips" which are delicious

Incredients

  • Potatoes
  • Fish Fillets
  • All-purpose Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • milk, eggs, salt, black pepper
  • Oil For Frying

Steps

  1. Peal and cut potatoes into strips
  2. Season potatoes, fish with salt and black pepper
  3. Mix flour with milk, baking powder, salt, eggs
  4. Heat oil to 180 degrees C
  5. Deep fry potatoes for 5 minutes
  6. Deep fish in the mixed-flour and fry for 10 minutes
  7. Serve and enjoy!
