Crème Brûlée

Description

This is a recipe to cook the simplest dessert 'Crème Brûlée'

Incredients

  • Haevy Cream
  • Egg yolks
  • Sugars(recommends brown sugar)
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees C
  2. Whisk together cream and 3 tablespoons sugar in bowl
  3. Cook in the microwave until warm, whisk with egg yolks and vanilla
  4. Pour cream mixture into ramekins
  5. Bake in the oven for about 30 minutes
  6. Take it out and chill in the refrigerator until cold
  7. Put some brown sugar on top and burn it before serving
