Pizza

Description

This is a recipe to cook classic pizza which are delicious

Incredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast, sugar, salt
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperonis
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220 degrees C
  2. Mix flour with warm olive oil, water, suga, yeast and salt to make pizza dough
  3. Put tomato sauce, mozzarella, pepperonis and oregano on top of the pizza dough
  4. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 20 minutes
  5. Take it out and enjoy it!
